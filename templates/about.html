Introduction

15 years into the housing business, Civil Homes has secured a place for itself as a leading real estate developer in Nepal. Built on its customers’ trust and support, Civil Homes strives to meet their ever-growing aspirations of a better living experience by bringing together the best of design, technology, and innovation in each project.

Since its first housing project in Bhainsepati or better known as Phase I, Civil Homes has already been successful in providing its ever-growing clientele base with three housing projects. Complete with aesthetic appeal, modern amenities, state-of- the-art architecture, and desired living standards, these projects sold out like hot cakes. And while the company continues the legacy with other colony projects, this Nepali real estate developer is spreading its wings and extending its expertise to retail malls and high rises.

Credited with introducing and pioneering the concept of housing, a concept that was once frowned upon in Nepal, Civil Homes believes in continuing the raise the bar higher, challenging the old, and introducing more innovative and apt living standards in the country. Driven by the same spirit, the company already has ground-breaking housing concepts in the pipeline – the Civil Luxury Residence in Sundhara and ‘Smart City’ in Panchkhal.

Manoeuvred by a strong management team, backed by years of experience and expertise, Civil Homes is headed towards its mission to offer value to its customers, shareholders, employees, and society at large. And while doing so, the company is also changing the level of professionalism, ethics, customer service, and contributing to Nepal’s socio-economic sphere.


Vision

To be a Successful conglomerate with diverse business enterprises.


Mission

TO offer value to our customers, shareholders, employees, and society at large.


Values

Perseverance, integrity, commitment.

Chairmain's Message

Hon. Er. Ichchha Raj Tamang

Chairman, Civil Group

In its seventeen years as a focused real estate development company, Civil homes Pvt. Ltd. has established a strong presence in the real estate market. The Civil Group sees its property development business as a major thrust area. The property development sector is already amongst the fastest growing in Nepal and in the coming decades, is likely to emerge as one of the largest in the country. Civil Homes firmly believes that it is uniquely positioned to take advantage of the increased need for excellence in real state.

I am proud of the fact that Civil Homes was the first Nepali real estate development company to have obtained as ISO certification. This is a manifestation of our quality process and our philosophy of putting customers’ interests first. Civil Homes is led by experienced and energetic professionals focused on the company’s core competencies. Together with our joint venture partners and associates, we have developed millions of square feet of quality real estate. Our unwavering commitment to improvement and innovation continues in the form of several signature projects currently underway.

Booking open for Phase VII Sitapaila

The site for this project is located at Dandapauwa VDC, Ramkot, Sitapaila, in close proximity with newly developed residential neighborhood, next to the Ring road from Bafal, and hence it’s a very good residential neighborhood. The housing complex comprises of about 118 units, among which 92 are individual bungalows and 26 are row house units, with plot areas ranging from 2.5 anas to 4 anas. Each dwelling unit is a class apart from all possible housing colonies as all requirements within a residence are meticulously planned and designed. The open plan for every unit promotes not just free admission of light and ventilation but also a compact plan with less building square footage and a world class residential ambiance.
