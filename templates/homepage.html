PHASE XI - Boudha

Bringing a cosmopolitan flair amidst the jungle- clad backdrop of Gokarna, Civil Homes’ Phase XI in Boudda,Jorpati gives new meaning to nature and nurture. Just 4.2 km away from the CHUCHEPATI BUS STOP, Boudhanath Sadak, the housing complex is built on soil-tested ground that is surrounded by hill tops on the north. Since the housing complex sits across a hilltop, it is surrounded by abundance of greenery and trees all around giving the residence complex a feel of retreat home.
